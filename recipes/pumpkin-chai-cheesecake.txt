From the 2010 Pillsbury Thanksgiving magazine
made 10/30/10

Crust:
2 cups crushed cinnamon graham crackers
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted

Filling:
4 8-oz packages cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
4 eggs
1 cup canned pumpkin
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
3/4 tsp ground cardamom

Instructions:
1. Heat oven to 300Â°F. Spray large pie pan with cooking spray.
2. In small bowl, mix crust ingredients. Press mixture in bottom and 1"
   up side of pan. Bake 8-10 min or until set. Cool 5 min.
3. In large bowl, beat softened cream cheese and granulated sugar with
   electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs,
   one at a time, just until blended.
4. To remaining cream cheese mixture, add pumpkin, pumpkin pie spice,
   and cardamom; mix with wire whisk until smooth. Spoon over mixture in
   pan.
5. Back 1 hr 15 min to 1 hr 25 min or until edge of cheesecake is set at
   least 2 inches from edge of pan but center of cheesecake still 
   jiggles slightly when moved. 
6. Turn oven off; open oven door at least 4". Let cheesecake remain in 
   oven 30 min. Cool in pan on cooling rack 30 more min. Cover loosely;
   refrigerate at least 6 hrs but no longer than 24 hrs.
